    Class ComRecord

    Represents a communication record, which is a call history entry stored for each conversation.

    Each record contains information about the call, including its unique identifier, reference, participants, timestamps, and acknowledgment status.

    A record may represent an incoming or outgoing call, and includes details such as the start and end times, as well as the conversation time when the call was answered.
