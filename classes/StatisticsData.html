    Class StatisticsData

    Represents the statistical data returned for a requester, including agent-level and pilot-level statistics grouped by time slot and observation period.

    A `StatisticsData` instance is returned by CallCenterStatistics.getDayData and CallCenterStatistics.getDaysData.
